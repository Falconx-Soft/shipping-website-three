Safety – Medicines

Safety and medicine are crucial aspects of ship chandling services, and Ship Chandler St Petersburg takes pride in providing high-quality safety equipment and medical supplies to vessels docked in the port of St Petersburg. These services are essential in ensuring the safety and well-being of the crew members on board.

Safety Equipment:

Safety is of utmost importance on board a ship, and Ship Chandler St Petersburg provides a wide range of safety equipment and supplies to ensure the vessel's safety. They offer life rafts, life jackets, fire extinguishers, and more to ensure that the vessel is prepared for any emergencies that may arise. The company understands the importance of reliable and high-quality safety equipment and works with trusted suppliers to provide the best possible products to their customers.
